How to Play

CLICK ME!!!
  • First sign up if you haven't. Next sign in and head to the game.
  • The game starts by default, so stay attentive.
  • You will have 5 lives, i.e. you can miss out 5 fruits to slice before the game overs!.
  • Just click anywhere on the screen and your knife is activated, cut as many fruits as possible.
  • Also beware that your knife (curser) doesn't even touch the bomb. The game will get over then!
  • Also remember, smaller the fruit, more the points!!!
  • You can also mute/unmute the music using the "m" key everywhere except the main menu!
  • HAPPY CUTTING...!!!
